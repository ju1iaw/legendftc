Our Story

We started competing in FLL in 2019, and FTC in 2022. Both times, our team had to learn the competitions from scratch and work our way up to becoming among California's best FIRST teams. In FTC, this process took us three seasons. We want other rookie teams to have an easier time integrating into the competitions and building amazing robots with resources that we found or would have found helpful.

Contact us with any questions, and we hope you find our content useful!
