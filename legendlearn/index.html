Our Story

We started competing in FLL in 2019, and FTC in 2022. We learned booking-mentorships competitions from scratch, working our way up from rookies to among California's strongest FIRST teams. In FTC, this process took us three seasons. We want other rookie teams to have an easier time integrating into competition robotics and building high-perfomerforming robots. That's what this site is for: book a free mentorship meeting, watch our tutorials, or email us directly!
