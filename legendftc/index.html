Legend is a 4th-year robotics team of middle and high schoolers based in Fremont, CA, competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. From entering the competition as rookies in 2022 to achieving our impressive global rankings in the 2025 season, we've grown as individuals and as a team through the competition. We proudly represent the values of FIRST in our community while maintaining robotics excellence.
