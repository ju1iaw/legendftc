Outreach & Community

Major Projects

STEM Education Initiative

Leading robotics workshops for local schools and community centers to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators.

Community Robotics Program

Partnering with local libraries and youth organizations to provide hands-on robotics experiences for underserved communities.

Mentorship Program

Mentoring younger robotics teams and providing technical guidance to help them succeed in competitions.

Environmental Awareness Campaign

Using robotics to promote environmental sustainability and educate the community about green technology solutions.

Regular Events

Monthly Workshops

Hosting monthly robotics workshops open to the public at our team headquarters.

School Demonstrations

Regular robot demonstrations at local schools to showcase the excitement of robotics and engineering.

Community Fairs

Participating in local community fairs and events to share our passion for robotics with the broader community.

Team Open Houses

Quarterly open house events where community members can tour our workspace and learn about our projects.

Online Tutorials

Creating and sharing educational content online to reach a wider audience interested in robotics.

Competition Support

Supporting and volunteering at local robotics competitions and events.
