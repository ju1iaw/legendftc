Legend is a 4th-year robotics team based in Fremont, CA, competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. From our rookie season (2022) to the present, we've grown as individuals and as a team through FIRST. Currently, we're ranked among the top teams in NorCal and the world. We strive to maintain robotics excellence and community impact with the values of FIRST.
