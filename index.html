Legend is a 3rd-year robotics team based in Fremont, CA competing in FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). From entering the competition as rookies in 2022 to achieving our impressive global rankings in 2025, we've grown as individuals and as a team through the competition. We proudly represent the values of FIRST and strive to leave a positive impact on our community while maintaining robotics excellence.
